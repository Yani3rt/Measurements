# Guidance Accent Design

## Goal

Introduce a contrasting accent color that helps guide attention without changing the core editorial palette of The Atelier.

## Approved Direction

Add a restrained petrol blue-green accent and reserve it for guidance states only.

- default structure stays navy
- measurement/archive language stays warm gold
- the new accent appears only when the interface needs to direct attention

## Accent Role

This accent is not a general brand color. It is a guidance signal.

Use it for:

- selected measurement emphasis
- active focus rings
- active ledger selection reinforcement
- subtle next-step or action-now cues

Avoid using it for:

- passive surfaces
- default controls
- general decorative fills
- recorded-state warmth

## Palette

- accent base: `#2E6F73`
- accent strong: `#24585C`
- accent soft: `#D7E8E6`

## Success Criteria

- the accent feels clearly distinct from the gold measurement language
- selected and guided states become easier to spot immediately
- the interface still feels editorial and calm rather than techy or dashboard-like
